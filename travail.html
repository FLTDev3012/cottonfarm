The work at the farm

A basic working day on the farm begins at 8 a.m. and ends between 5 p.m. and 8 p.m. There are two types of days, picking days and non-picking days. The picking day is a day that will mainly take place in the cotton fields, where you will be on board your builder, to carry out the main task in our farm, the pressing of the cotton. On non-picking days, you will be offered different tasks, such as paint cleaning or DIY.

Cotton pressing

Your main job at Condamine Park will be pressing cotton on module builder. You will wait for the pickers who come back and forth to bring you the cotton.

The Tarps

When you have finished a Module, you will then have to cover it with a Tarp to keep the cotton dry.

cotton picking

Sometimes it will be necessary to pick up the cotton fallen from the module builders or the pickers

Mechanical

Do-it-yourself or mechanical activities may be required of you, such as painting, cleaning or tinkering if you had the appropriate skills
